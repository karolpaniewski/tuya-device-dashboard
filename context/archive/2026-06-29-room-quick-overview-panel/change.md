## Summary

Replace the centered `RoomModal` dialog on the dashboard with a slide-in Sheet panel (420px, right side) that keeps the dashboard visible in the background. The panel shows: current temperature + badge, 24h temperature chart (hidden if no sensor), device list with inline status, automation modes list, and a heat toggle. The `RoomModal` in the automation-flow canvas is preserved unchanged.
