Extend the automation flow canvas from a single-room view to a full-topology
view (all modes × all rooms), then add bulk-connect and bulk-disconnect: double-
click a mode node to activate it, shift-click or lasso-select multiple room
nodes, then click "Connect N" / "Disconnect M" to apply the batch operation.
